# 🎓 Girls Education Platform

Empowering girls through education, awareness, and digital support.

🌐 Live Website: https://sanchi-girls.vercel.app/

---

## 🚀 About the Project

The **Girls Education Platform** is a web-based initiative designed to support and empower girls by providing access to:

- 📚 Educational resources  
- 🩺 Health & hygiene awareness  
- 🤖 AI-based guidance and assistance  
- 📊 Personal tracking tools  

Our goal is to create a **safe, informative, and accessible digital space** for girls to grow, learn, and stay informed.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 AI Assistance
- Smart chatbot for guidance
- Answers related to health, education, and daily concerns

### 💧 Health & Hygiene Hub
- Daily wellness tracking  
- Hygiene tips and reminders  
- Period and health awareness  

### 📊 Personal Tracking
- Track habits like:
  - Water intake  
  - Sleep cycle  
  - Daily routines  

### 📚 Educational Support
- Access to important learning content  
- Awareness about opportunities and growth
